Our Story

Established in 2007, The Gracehouse Resort has endeavored to be the hallmark of assurance for discriminating guests when choosing quality accommodation.


All aspects of comfort and personalized service to our guests are not only upheld, but constantly enhanced, making us the preferred Guest House in Nairobi.


Our Location

Of significance is the location of Gracehouse Resort, Adjacent to Yaya centre mall (one of the top ranking malls in Africa). Located in kilimani, away from the hustle and bustle of Nairobi city centre, yet only 5km from the CBD and 18km from Jomo Kenyatta International Airport.

Gracehouse Resort is also a walking distance from Nakumart Presitge and Junction on the Ngong Road, has adequate access from Argwings Khodek Road and Ring Road Kilimani, makes Gracehouse Resort location the most convenient and accessible place to stay.
